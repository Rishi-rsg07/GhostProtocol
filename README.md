# GhostProtocol
A SaaS that intentionally, randomly, and safely breaks your staging or production environments to test your engineering team's real-time observability, alerting, and incident response.
Think Chaos Engineering (like Netflix's Chaos Monkey), but packaged specifically as a gamified training and compliance tool for mid-sized startups. Instead of just testing if the servers survive, it tests if the humans and their documentation survive.
Most software promises to keep things running perfectly. This software charges people money to break their systems on purpose, scoring the team on "Time to Acknowledge" and "Time to Resolution.
